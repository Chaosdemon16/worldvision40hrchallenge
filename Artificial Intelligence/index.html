Artificial intellegence

What is Artificial Intellegence?

Artificial intellegence is a field of science that focuses on making computers that can think and use human reason. AI uses complicated algorythms to formulate human-like responses to prompts

Is AI a good thing or a bad thing?

While AI isn't inherently good or bad it is certainly revolutionising how we go about our lives, Whether it's friendly chatbots on your favorite websites or bots scanning data to find inaccuracies, AI is everywhere in our daily lives.

Is AI really sentient?

Not yet, AI technology has not yet reached sentience and there are groups actively trying to prevent AI sentience because of fears for humanity after how some people treat AI
