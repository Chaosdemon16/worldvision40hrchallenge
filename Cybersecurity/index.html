Staying safe online

  • Protect your information.

    Don't share your private information such as passwords online

  • Block & Report.

    if you or someone you know is being harrased online then you can report the person to the site moderator and block them from being able to view your profile.

  • Use a VPN.

    Even if you use incognito mode on your devices your ISP (Internet service provider) can still see what you are looking at and they can sell that information to advertising agencies who use it to give you targeted ads

  • Don't be a Bully.

    Remeber, Even though it's all digital, you are talking to real people with real feelings. If you wouldn't say it in person, then don't say it online
