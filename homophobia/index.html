Homophobia & LGBTQ+ Violence

Homophobia is the culuturally produced fear or prejudice againt homosexuals. It commonly manifests itself in laws meant to restrict the ability for gay people to live their lives. Often, Homophobia and anti-LGBTQ+ sentiment translates itself into bullying and violence against members of the gay and LGBTQ+ community a.k.a gay bashing.


Religious motivations

Anti gay and LGBTQ sentiment is most commonly from religious or cultural pressures. In christianity and islam homosexual intercourse is considered sinful. Regardless of religion though, Homophobia and gay bashing are still terrible acts of violence.
