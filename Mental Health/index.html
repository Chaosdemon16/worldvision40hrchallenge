Mental Health

What is Mental Health?

Mental Health is the term used to describe a persons overall emotional and psycological state. For some people, Mental health can refer to mental illnesses such as Bipolar. but in reality everyone needs to take care of their mental health.

Mental health support

If you are struggling with anxiety or deppression you can reach out to 0800 111 757 for depression support or 1737 for anxiety support.

How to take care of your mental health

  • Take Walks; even just 30mins of walking a day can help improve your mood
  • Make sure you sleep well; Its important to have a regular sleep schedule to make sure you get enough sleep
  • Stay positive; Think about what's going well for you rather than focusing on negitives.
