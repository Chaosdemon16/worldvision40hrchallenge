Free Speech

Free speech is the right to state your opinion, share information, disagree with those in power and to express your opinions in peaceful protests. Freedom of speech and freedom of expression are protected under article 19 of the United Nations Declaration of Human Rights.


Censorship

Despite it being protected in international law many governments around the world violate their citizens' freedom of expression by imprisoning people - or worse - that dare speak out against them. The countries that completely control the media like Russia and China also significantly restrict the freedom of information within their borders.


Hate Speech

Many right wing individuals and groups excuse their hate speech as them excercising their freedom of speech. Despite freedom of speech - even that which is deeply offensive - being protected under international law, speech that violates the rights of others, or advocates hatred and incites discrimination or violence isn't protected.
