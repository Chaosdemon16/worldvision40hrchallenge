Domestic Violence Prevention

violent or aggressive behaviour within the home, typically involving the violent abuse of a spouse or partner

What is domestic violence?

Domestic violence, also known as Intimate partner abuse is abuse from someone in the victims domestic circle ie a spouse, partner or parents

The Facts

  • In NZ its estimated that only 33% of domestic violence is reported
  • The police attend an incident relating to domestic violence every 4 minutes
  • In 2021-2022 16% of police frontline activity was related to domestic violence
  • Homosexual and bisexual adults experience intimate partner abuse twice as much as other New Zealanders
  • 1 in 3 kiwi women report being the victim of ipa compared to 1 in 8 men
