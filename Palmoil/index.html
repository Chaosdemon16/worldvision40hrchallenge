Palm Oil

What is Palm Oil?

Palm oil is a vegedable oil made by crushing the fruit of the palm oil tree, there are two types of palm oil; crude palm oil - made by squeezing the fruit - and Palm kernal oil - made by crushing the kernal of the fruit.

What is Palm oil used for?

Palm oil is in almost 50% of the food in supermarkets; things like toothpaste, doughnuts and deodorant. Its also used as fuel and to feed animals

The Problem with Palm Oil

Palm oil farming has, and continues to be one of the major drivers of deforsiation. Palm oil farming destroys the habitats of endagered speices like Orangatangs, Pigmy Elephants and the Sumatran Rhino.

The Solutions to Palm Oil

The Roundtableo on sustainable palm oil (RSPO) is a group formed in 2004 to address increasing concerns around the impact of palm oil on society.
